It is to Professor Samuel F. B. Morse, the distinguished inventor of the Magnetic Telegraph, of New York, that we are indebted for the application of photography to portrait taking. He was in Paris, for the purpose of presenting to the scientific world his Electro Magnetic Telegraph, at the time M. Daguerre announced his splendid discovery; its astounding results, as well as its bearing on the art of design claimed his attention.
